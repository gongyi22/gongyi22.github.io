WHO AM I

I'm a Shanghainese Communication Designer who had been living in Melbourne for 5 years and graduated from Monash University. I love everything that has to do with design & development, such as Graphic design,Entity identity, UI, Web design, Motion design etc. I feel a true devotion for Typography, Graphic design and UI design. I have 5 years of experience working as a freelance and working at some studios, and I’m also ready for visiting other countries and working for any design studio or agency! I love working with people, specially if they have the same passion I have for what they do.

WHAT CAN I DO

For Brand identity, I have extensive experience in working on large-scale brand identity projects. This means fluency in moving from understanding and interpreting strategy to developing initial concepts and creating brand guidelines to guiding implementation across a range of different applications for digital, space and print.

For Editorial design, I understand what’s needed to effectively and compellingly bring content to life having worked on books, manuals, magazines and printed publications.

For Information graphics, I make sense of complex org charts, business flows and data and ability to present information in an easy to understand and memorable way.

For Typography, I have a love and obsession for the finer details of type and knowledge of how to express emotions and evoke reactions through type.

For Packaging Design, excellent understanding of form, structure, and materials, and ability to articulate clear information hierarchy and great design on anything from food products to electronic goods.

For Exhibition graphics & signage, I can communicating in space to create clear and immersive visual journeys for retail spaces, events or exhibitions.

For Production knowledge, I have solid knowledge in production and artworking to liaise with suppliers and vendors in producing exquisitely executed pieces of print design.

For Languages, I also am a bilingual designers with fluency in written and spoken English and Mandarin.
