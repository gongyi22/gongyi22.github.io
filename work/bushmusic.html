Melbourne Bush Music Festival Poster

The brief requests to design a illustrated poster for the upcoming music festival in Melbourne, Australia. The Bush Music Festival is one of the biggest folk music fastival in Meblourne and the folk music is the main stream of this wonderful festival. Therefore, in order to express the sense of nature and the spirits of this festival, I decided to use illustrated bush and "spirits" as our theme of the poster and combined with a night scene and a vintage texture on a A1 size 500GSM paper.
